What We Offer

Care designed
for every stage
of life.

From annual wellness visits to complex specialist coordination, Acme Health Practice delivers a full spectrum of medical services under one roof — so your care is seamless, connected, and complete.

01
Primary Care

Your first point of contact for all health concerns. Our primary care physicians provide ongoing, comprehensive care including annual physicals, illness management, and health monitoring.

02
Specialist Referrals

When specialized expertise is needed, we coordinate seamlessly with our trusted network of specialists — ensuring warm handoffs and continuous communication throughout your care journey.

03
Preventive Care

Proactive health means fewer surprises. We offer age-appropriate screenings, immunizations, lifestyle counseling, and personalized wellness plans to keep you ahead of potential health concerns.

04
Telehealth

Quality care from wherever you are. Our secure video consultations connect you with your physician for follow-ups, medication reviews, and non-urgent concerns — no commute required.

Our Story

Medicine with
intention
and integrity.

Founded in 2001, Acme Health Practice was built on a simple belief: every patient deserves a physician who truly listens. Over two decades later, that principle guides every appointment, every diagnosis, and every care decision we make.

Patient-First Philosophy

Every clinical decision starts with what matters most to you — your goals, your values, your life.

Evidence-Based Medicine

Our clinical protocols are grounded in the latest research and continuously updated as science advances.

Community Partnership

We are proud members of this community — participating in local health initiatives and welcoming all patients.
